# Chennai jobs — digital PR and backlinks

Earned links support organic visibility for the jobs hub and guide. This is **operator work** (email, partnerships, community posts); the repo only documents URLs and angles.

## URLs to share

| URL | Use when |
|-----|----------|
| `/chennai-jobs` | “Where to browse open roles we list for Chennai.” |
| `/guides/chennai-tech-careers` | “How to read ads, red flags, and Chennai hiring context.” |

Use the full production origin (e.g. `https://mychennaicity.in/chennai-jobs`) in outreach.

## Pitch angles (plain, not salesy)

- **Local + human-checked:** Small set of Chennai-focused listings; not a national firehose.
- **Reader guide:** Helps candidates parse titles, salary silence, and hybrid/OMR language.
- **News tie-in:** Economy and local reporting on the same site — good for college career cells and community newsletters.

## Target groups

- Chennai / Tamil Nadu **chambers** and SME networks.
- **College** placement cells and tech clubs (guest note or “resources” page link).
- **Startup** and IT **Slack/Discord/WhatsApp** communities (only where links are allowed; no spam).
- **Newsletter** editors for city or tech roundups.

## What not to do

- Paid link schemes or bulk directory spam.
- Identical copy-paste emails — personalize with why the guide fits their audience.

## Measurement

- In GSC, track **referring domains** indirectly via new discovery queries and landing pages for `/chennai-jobs` and the guide.
- Note partnership URLs in a simple spreadsheet (org, date, URL placed) for follow-up.
